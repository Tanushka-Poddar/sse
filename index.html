About Us

Why Choose Us?

We provide top notch quality services for our customers

Established in 1991, Sri Shyam Electronics was envisioned to become the largest providers of all types of IT products in West Bengal with some other states also. Today we still strive in growing dynamically overcoming all the challenges that come our way, and feel pride when the market recognizes us as an accomplished dealer in IT products and Solutions. We share a very strong relationship with all our clients. We deal in around 200 brands and around 3000 products which is expanding day by day as per the latest updated technologies and market demands. We have always came forward with all the latest technologies in IT sector and still moving ahead. We are completely dedicated to our customers and there product satisfaction. We have well trained Sales and Marketing Executives who not only deal with customers but also understand their need and provide the solution to them..
